PAV BHAJI

Description

Pav Bhaji is a popular street food dish from India, particularly famous in Mumbai. It consists of a spicy vegetable mash (bhaji) served with buttered bread rolls (pav). The bhaji is made from a mix of mashed vegetables cooked in a flavorful blend of spices, creating a delicious and satisfying meal that is enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients Required To Make Delicious Pav Bhaji

  • 3 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup cauliflower, chopped and boiled
  • 1 cup green peas, boiled
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), finely chopped
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
  • 8 pav (bread rolls), lightly toasted with butter
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Steps To Make Pav Bhaji

  1. Prepare Vegetables: Boil potatoes, cauliflower, and green peas until soft. Mash the potatoes and roughly mash the cauliflower and peas.
  2. Sauté Onion and Spices: In a pan, heat oil and butter. Add chopped onions, ginger-garlic paste, and green chilies. Sauté until onions are golden brown.
  3. Add Vegetables and Spices: Add chopped tomatoes, bell pepper, pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the vegetables are soft and well combined with the spices.
  4. Mash and Simmer: Mash the vegetable mixture using a masher or back of a spoon. Add water as needed to achieve the desired consistency. Simmer for a few minutes until flavors meld.
  5. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves. Serve hot pav bhaji with lightly toasted pav (bread rolls), lemon wedges, and additional butter if desired. Enjoy the spicy and flavorful pav bhaji!
