BUTTER CHICKEN

Description

Butter chicken, also known as murgh makhani, is a popular Indian dish characterized by its rich, creamy, and mildly spiced tomato-based sauce. It typically consists of marinated chicken that is first grilled or roasted, then cooked in a fragrant blend of butter, cream, and aromatic spices such as garam masala, cumin, coriander, and fenugreek. The sauce often includes ingredients like tomatoes, onions, garlic, ginger, and a touch of sweetness from honey or sugar. Butter chicken is traditionally served with naan, rice, or other Indian bread, making it a beloved and comforting dish in Indian cuisine.

Ingredients Required To Make Delicious Butter Chicken

  • 3 tablespoons butter (divided)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit (optional, adjust to taste)
  • 2 cups tomato purée (fresh or canned)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
  • 2 tablespoons oil or butter
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Steps To Make Butter Chicken

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, salt, and lemon juice. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Sauté the marinated chicken in oil or butter until browned and cooked through. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the Sauce: In the same pan, melt butter and sauté chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies until golden brown.
  4. Cook the Tomato Masala: Add tomato purée and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Finish the Sauce: Stir in heavy cream and kasuri methi. Simmer until thickened.
  6. Combine Chicken and Sauce: Add the cooked chicken to the sauce. Cook for 5-10 minutes. Adjust consistency with water if needed.
  7. Adjust Seasoning: Season with salt and add honey or sugar if needed.
  8. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve hot with naan, rice, or other Indian bread. Optional: Serve with lemon wedges.
