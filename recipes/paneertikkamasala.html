PANEER TIKKA MASALA

Description

Paneer Tikka Masala is a popular Indian dish that features grilled paneer cubes cooked in a rich and flavorful tomato-based sauce. The paneer is marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture and then grilled to perfection before being simmered in the creamy masala sauce. This dish is a favorite among vegetarians and is often served with rice or Indian breads.

Ingredients Required To Make Delicious Paneer Tikka Masala

  • 250g paneer, cut into cubes
  • For Marinade:
    • 1/2 cup yogurt
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • Salt to taste
  • For Masala Sauce:
    • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, pureed
    • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1/2 cup heavy cream
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Steps To Make Paneer Tikka Masala

  1. Marinate Paneer: In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Add paneer cubes and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Grill Paneer: Preheat grill or oven. Thread marinated paneer onto skewers and grill until lightly charred. Set aside.
  3. Prepare Masala Sauce: In a pan, heat oil or ghee. Add cumin seeds and sauté until they splutter. Add chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Cook Tomatoes and Spices: Add tomato puree, tomato paste, garam masala, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add Cream and Paneer: Stir in heavy cream and add grilled paneer cubes. Simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens and paneer is coated well.
  6. Adjust Consistency and Seasoning: If needed, adjust the consistency with water. Taste and adjust salt and spices as per preference.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves. Serve hot with rice or Indian breads. Enjoy the aromatic and delicious Paneer Tikka Masala!
