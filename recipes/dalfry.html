DAL FRY

Description

Dal Fry is a popular Indian lentil dish made with cooked lentils tempered with aromatic spices. It is a flavorful and nutritious dish that is often served as a side dish with rice or Indian bread. Dal Fry can be made using various types of lentils such as yellow lentils (moong dal), red lentils (masoor dal), or split chickpeas (chana dal), and it is known for its rich and comforting taste.

Ingredients Required To Make Delicious Dal Fry

  • 1 cup lentils (yellow lentils, red lentils, or split chickpeas)
  • 3 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2 green chilies, slit (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Steps To Make Dal Fry

  1. Cook Lentils: Rinse lentils and cook them in water until soft. Set aside.
  2. Temper Spices: In a pan, heat ghee or oil and add cumin seeds. Sauté until they splutter.
  3. Sauté Onions and Aromatics: Add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until onions are golden brown.
  4. Add Tomatoes and Spices: Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft and oil separates.
  5. Combine Lentils and Spices: Add cooked lentils to the pan and mix well with the spice mixture. Cook for a few minutes to blend flavors.
  6. Adjust Consistency and Seasoning: If needed, add water to adjust the consistency. Taste and adjust salt and spices as per preference.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot with rice or Indian bread. Optional: Serve with lemon wedges for extra flavor.
