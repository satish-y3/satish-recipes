CHAI

Description

Chai, also known as masala chai, is a traditional Indian tea made with a blend of spices and herbs. It is typically brewed with black tea leaves, milk, sugar, and a combination of spices such as cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and ginger. Chai is known for its aromatic and flavorful profile, making it a popular beverage enjoyed hot or cold.

Ingredients Required To Make Delicious Chai

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 teaspoons black tea leaves (or 4 tea bags)
  • 4-5 green cardamom pods, crushed
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 whole cloves
  • Sugar or sweetener to taste

Steps To Make Chai

  1. Boil Water and Spices: In a saucepan, bring water to a boil. Add crushed cardamom pods, grated ginger, cinnamon stick, and whole cloves. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add Tea Leaves: Add black tea leaves (or tea bags) to the boiling water and spices. Let it simmer for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add Milk: Pour in milk and stir well. Simmer for 3-4 minutes or until the chai reaches your desired strength.
  4. Strain and Sweeten: Strain the chai into cups to remove the spices and tea leaves. Add sugar or sweetener to taste and stir until dissolved.
  5. Serve: Pour the chai into cups and enjoy hot. Optionally, garnish with a sprinkle of ground cinnamon or cardamom on top.
