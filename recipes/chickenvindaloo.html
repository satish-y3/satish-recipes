CHICKEN VINDALOO

Description

Chicken Vindaloo is a spicy and tangy curry dish originating from Goa, India. It is known for its bold flavors and fiery red color, achieved from a blend of spices including red chili peppers, vinegar, and garlic. Chicken pieces are marinated and then simmered in a rich and flavorful sauce, creating a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients Required To Make Delicious Chicken Vindaloo

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 3 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit (adjust to taste)
  • 2 teaspoons mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 2 cups tomato purée (fresh or canned)
  • 1 cup water or chicken stock
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
  • Steamed rice or bread for serving
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Steps To Make Chicken Vindaloo

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Mix chicken with turmeric powder, red chili powder, vinegar, and salt. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. Sauté Onions and Spices: In a pan, heat oil or ghee and sauté onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies, mustard seeds, and cumin seeds until onions are golden brown.
  3. Cook the Masala: Add tomato purée, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add Chicken and Simmer: Add marinated chicken pieces and water or chicken stock. Cover and simmer until chicken is cooked through and the sauce thickens.
  5. Adjust Seasoning: Taste and adjust salt and spice levels if needed.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves. Serve hot with steamed rice or bread. Optional: Serve with lemon wedges for extra tanginess.
