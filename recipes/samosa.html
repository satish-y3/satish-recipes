SAMOSA

Description

Samosa is a popular Indian snack that features crispy, golden-brown pastry filled with a savory potato and pea mixture. It is a delicious and satisfying snack enjoyed by people around the world. Samosas are often served with tamarind chutney or mint chutney, making them a flavorful treat for any occasion.

Ingredients Required To Make Delicious Samosas

  • For Dough:
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • Cold water as needed
  • For Filling:
    • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
    • 1/2 cup green peas, boiled
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 green chilies, chopped (adjust to taste)
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • Salt to taste
    • 2 tablespoons oil for frying

Steps To Make Samosas

  1. Prepare Dough: In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, oil, and salt. Gradually add cold water and knead into a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Make Filling: In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Sauté until they splutter. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until onions are translucent.
  3. Add Spices and Potatoes: Add garam masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well. Add mashed potatoes, boiled peas, and lemon juice. Mix until combined. Remove from heat and let the filling cool.
  4. Shape Samosas: Divide the dough into equal-sized balls. Roll each ball into a thin oval shape. Cut in half to make semi-circles. Fold each semi-circle into a cone shape, fill with the potato filling, and seal the edges using water.
  5. Fry Samosas: Heat oil in a deep pan for frying. Once hot, fry the samosas in batches until golden and crispy. Drain excess oil on paper towels.
  6. Serve: Serve hot samosas with tamarind chutney or mint chutney. Enjoy the crispy and flavorful snack!
