ORANGE JUICE

Description

Orange juice is a refreshing and nutritious drink made from fresh oranges. It is rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, making it a healthy choice for boosting immunity and overall well-being. Freshly squeezed orange juice is simple to make and is a perfect beverage for breakfast or any time of the day.

Ingredients Required To Make Fresh Orange Juice

  • 4-6 large oranges
  • Optional: Sugar or sweetener to taste
  • Optional: Ice cubes for serving
  • Optional: Mint leaves or orange slices for garnish

Steps To Make Orange Juice

  1. Prepare Oranges: Wash the oranges thoroughly under running water. Cut them in half.
  2. Squeeze Oranges: Using a citrus juicer or a manual juicing tool, squeeze the oranges to extract the juice. Remove any seeds if present.
  3. Sweeten (Optional): Taste the orange juice and add sugar or sweetener if desired. Stir well to dissolve.
  4. Serve: Pour the orange juice into glasses. Add ice cubes if preferred. Garnish with mint leaves or orange slices for an attractive presentation.
  5. Enjoy: Serve the freshly made orange juice immediately for the best flavor and nutritional benefits. Cheers to a healthy and refreshing drink!
