PALAK PANEER

Description

Palak Paneer is a classic Indian dish made with spinach (palak) and paneer (Indian cottage cheese). It is a nutritious and flavorful dish that combines the goodness of spinach with the richness of paneer, seasoned with aromatic spices. Palak Paneer is often enjoyed with rice or Indian breads like naan or roti.

Ingredients Required To Make Delicious Palak Paneer

  • 250g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 4 cups spinach (palak), washed and chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Steps To Make Palak Paneer

  1. Blanch Spinach: Boil water in a pot and blanch the chopped spinach for 2-3 minutes. Drain and immediately transfer to cold water. Blend to a smooth puree.
  2. Sauté Onion and Spices: In a pan, heat oil or ghee. Add cumin seeds and sauté until they splutter. Add chopped onion, ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Sauté until onions are golden brown.
  3. Add Tomatoes and Spices: Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft and oil separates.
  4. Cook Spinach Puree: Add the spinach puree and cook for a few minutes until well combined with the spices.
  5. Add Paneer and Cream: Add paneer cubes and fresh cream. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until paneer is heated through and absorbs the flavors.
  6. Adjust Consistency and Seasoning: If needed, adjust the consistency with water. Taste and adjust salt and spices as per preference.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves. Serve hot with rice or Indian breads. Enjoy the creamy and flavorful Palak Paneer!
