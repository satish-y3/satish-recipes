CHICKEN TIKKA MASALA

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a flavorful and popular Indian dish made with marinated and grilled chicken pieces cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce. It is typically seasoned with a blend of aromatic spices such as garam masala, cumin, coriander, and paprika. Chicken Tikka Masala is often served with rice or naan, making it a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients Required To Make Delicious Chicken Tikka Masala

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2 cups tomato purée (fresh or canned)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
  • Naan or rice for serving
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Steps To Make Chicken Tikka Masala

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, spices, and salt. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Grill the Chicken: Grill or bake the marinated chicken until cooked and slightly charred. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the Sauce: In a pan, heat oil or ghee and sauté chopped onions, garlic, and ginger until golden brown.
  4. Cook the Tomato Sauce: Add tomato purée, garam masala, cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add Cream and Chicken: Stir in heavy cream, kasuri methi, and honey or sugar. Add grilled chicken pieces and simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Adjust Seasoning: Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves. Serve hot with naan or rice. Optional: Serve with lemon wedges.
